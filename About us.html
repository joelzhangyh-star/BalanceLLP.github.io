About Us

At Balance Works LLP, we are a team of passionate qualified accounting professionals who believe that accounting should empower — not overwhelm. We specialize in supporting startups and SMEs with flexible, affordable services that adapt to your unique journey.

Whether you're launching a new venture or scaling your operations, we are here to simplify your finances, keep you compliant, and help you grow with confidence. Our approach blends precision with empathy, because we know that behind every business is a person striving for balance.

Let us handle the numbers, so you can focus on what truly matters.

Our Mission

Balance Works LLP exists to empower startups and SMEs with agile, cost-effective accounting solutions — while advancing human-centered social impact through inclusive employment. We create dignified work opportunities for retirees, caregivers, and individuals facing barriers to re-entry, fostering financial clarity and personal renewal.

Our Vision

To redefine the role of accounting in society — not only as a tool for business growth, but as a platform for equity, flexibility, and purpose. We envision a future where financial services uplift communities, restore confidence, and enable every individual to thrive through meaningful work.

Our Core Values

Purpose-Driven Precision

We deliver accurate, timely financial insights that empower confident decisions and sustainable growth.
